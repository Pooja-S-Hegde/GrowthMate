## 2. AI Pricing Advisor

### Overview
Pricing products correctly can be the difference between losing money and maximizing profit. The **AI Pricing Advisor** analyzes your costs, competitor pricing, and current market demand to recommend the exact price point you should sell your product or service at.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Navigate** to the **Pricing** tab in your dashboard.
2. **Fill out the Business Form:**
   - **Cost Price (₹):** How much it costs you to acquire or produce the item.
   - **Current Selling Price (₹):** What you currently charge your customers.
   - **Competitor Price (₹):** What your main competitors charge for a similar item.
   - **Monthly Sales Volume:** Roughly how many units you sell per month.
   - **Demand Level:** Select from Low, Medium, or High. (If everyone is asking for it, select High).
   - **Revenue Trend:** Select whether your overall sales are Increasing, Stable, or Decreasing.
3. Click the **Get Pricing Recommendation** button. 

### Understanding Your Results
Once the AI finishes analyzing (which takes a few seconds), it will present:
- **Recommended Price:** The exact price you should list your item at to balance volume and profit.
- **Profit Margin:** The expected margin at this recommended price.
- **Risk Level:** Shown as Low, Medium, or High. A "High" risk means your price might alienate customers or be too close to your cost price.
- **Strategic Advice:** A detailed explanation from the AI explaining *why* it chose this price and how you should position it in the market.
- **Minimum Safe Price:** Never sell below this number, or you will likely take a loss considering overheads.

## 3. DealFixer: AI vs AI Negotiation

### Overview
**DealFixer** is a powerful simulation tool. It pits an AI Buyer against an AI Seller in real-time. You can use this to figure out how much customers might haggle, or to train your sales team on how to defend product value against tough negotiators.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Navigate** to the **Negotiation** tab.
2. **Configure the Negotiation:**
   - **Product Name:** Be specific (e.g., "Used 2021 MacBook Pro M1", "Bulk Supply of Office Chairs").
   - **Market Price (Optional):** If you leave this blank, the AI will automatically estimate the market value for you. If you put in a price (e.g., 50000), the AI will use this as the baseline.
3. Click **Start Negotiation Battle**.

### Interpreting the Simulation
- **The Chat Window:** You will see messages pop up in real-time. 
  - The **Blue Chat Bubbles (AI Buyer)** will try to lowball, ask for discounts, and point out market alternatives.
  - The **Green Chat Bubbles (AI Seller)** will defend the price, highlight features, and offer minor concessions.
- **Rounds:** They will negotiate for up to 10 rounds.
- **Final Result:** At the end, you will see either a **Deal Secured!** (with the final agreed-upon price) or **No Agreement** (meaning the buyer walked away). 
- **How to Use This:** Take the final agreed price as a realistic expectation for what a human customer might settle at after haggling.

## 4. Marketing Studio

### Overview
Hiring a marketing agency is expensive. The **Marketing Studio** acts as your personal marketing director and graphic designer. Give it your business details, and it will generate a complete campaign.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Navigate** to the **Marketing** tab.
2. **Enter Business Details:**
   - **Business Name & Product:** What are you selling?
   - **Category:** Select your industry (e.g., Electronics, Services).
   - **Target Audience:** Be descriptive! (e.g., "College students looking for affordable laptops").
   - **Location:** Where are you selling? (e.g., "Downtown Chicago", "Online globally").
   - **Upload Logo (Optional):** Attach your business logo if you want it referenced.
3. Click **Generate Strategy**.

### Utilizing Your Marketing Assets
After a few moments, the AI will output a full suite of materials:
- **Campaign Strategy:** The big idea. It tells you what kind of offer to run (e.g., "Flash Sale", "Bundle Deal") and for how long.
- **WhatsApp Blast:** A highly converting, emoji-rich message you can copy directly and send to your customer broadcast lists.
- **Instagram Post:** A ready-to-post caption complete with generated hashtags.
- **20-Sec Ad Script:** A script you can read off a teleprompter for an Instagram Reel or TikTok video.
- **AI Generated Poster:** A high-quality, AI-designed commercial poster for your campaign. 
  - You can click **Download** to save it to your computer.
  - Click the **Expand** icon to view it in full screen.
  - Use the **Share** button to send it directly to your social media or team members.

## 5. Sentiment Intelligence (Customer Insights)

### Overview
Unhappy customers leave bad reviews that hurt your business. **Sentiment Intelligence** reads customer complaints, assesses the risk to your business, and *automatically* sends a highly professional, de-escalating apology email on your behalf.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Navigate** to the **Insights** tab.
2. **Enter Customer Details:**
   - **Customer Name & Email:** (Crucial! The system needs the email to send the auto-reply).
3. **Set the Context:**
   - Enter your **Store Name**.
   - Select your **Category**, **Revenue Trend**, and current **Complaint Frequency**.
4. **Paste the Message:** Copy and paste the angry (or happy!) email/message the customer sent you into the text box.
5. Click **Analyze & Dispatch Reply**.

### Understanding Risk and Auto-Replies
- **Sentiment & Severity:** The system instantly reads the emotional tone. If the customer is furious, it marks it as "High Severity".
- **Churn Risk:** Tells you the likelihood that this customer will never buy from you again.
- **Automatic Dispatch:** The moment you click analyze, the AI drafts a tailored, empathetic response and *automatically emails the customer*. You will see a "Resolution Acknowledged" success screen if the email sent correctly.
- **Mitigation Strategy:** The AI gives you internal advice—such as whether you should offer them a 20% discount coupon or simply apologize—and how to fix the root cause of the problem long-term.

## 6. Business Orchestrator

### Overview
If you're not sure what part of your business needs attention today, use the **Business Orchestrator**. It takes a macro view of your company and tells you exactly what to prioritize.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Navigate** to the **Orchestrator** tab.
2. **Set Your Business State:**
   - **Revenue Trend:** Are you growing or declining?
   - **Profit Margin (%):** Enter your overall average margin.
   - **Competitor Position:** Are you more expensive, average, or cheaper than rivals?
   - **Customer Sentiment:** Are customers generally happy or unhappy?
3. Click **Run Analysis**.

### Interpreting the "Business Health"
- **Business Health:** The AI gives you a clear grade (Good, Moderate, or Risky).
- **Activate Service:** The AI will tell you which tool to use next. For example, if revenues are declining and sentiment is low, it might say: *"High Priority: Activate Marketing Studio for a Retention Campaign."*
- **Immediate Action:** A quick 1-sentence instruction on what you should do today to improve your business health.

## 7. Troubleshooting & Support

- **Tools are loading endlessly:** Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, the AI servers might be experiencing high traffic. Please try again in a few minutes.
- **Email failed to send in Sentiment Intelligence:** This usually means the email address entered was formatted incorrectly, or there is a temporary issue with your company's mail server integration. Please try again, or manually copy the suggested response if needed.
- **Poster Generation failed:** AI image generation is complex. If it fails, simply click the "Retry Generation" button that appears on the screen.
